About Us


Welcome to CreativeTee! This business has a range of graphic t-shirts. We're dedicated to giving you the very best quality, trendy yet affordable t-shirt.

Founded in 2014 by Ling Kan, CreativeTee has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office in London. When Ling first started out , her passion for creative new and innovative fashion ideas, drove her to come up with new ideas for customers to have stylish t-shirts. We now serve customers all over Britain, and are thrilled to be a part of the fashion industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
